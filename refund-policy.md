# Refund Policy for Explainer

**Last updated: April 30, 2026**

## Subscription Model

Explainer offers paid subscriptions processed securely by **Paddle** (our Merchant of Record). Paddle handles all billing, payments, and refunds on our behalf.

## Refund Eligibility

- **14-day money-back guarantee** — You may request a full refund within 14 days of your initial subscription purchase.
- Refund requests made after 14 days will not be eligible unless required by applicable law.
- Refunds are not issued for partial subscription periods or unused time remaining in a billing cycle.

## How to Request a Refund

Contact Paddle support directly at [paddle.com/support](https://paddle.com/support) with your transaction details.

## Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation stops future charges but does not trigger an automatic refund for the current billing period. To cancel, manage your subscription via the link provided in your Paddle receipt email.

## Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed by Paddle within **5–10 business days** and returned to your original payment method.
